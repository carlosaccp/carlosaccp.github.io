I was teaching assistant for STAT5264 Stochastic Processes with Applications, a master's level course which is mandatory for students in the [Mathematics of Finance](https://www.math.columbia.edu/mafn/) program at Columbia Univesity. The instructor was [Prof. Graeme Baker](https://sites.google.com/view/graemebaker). Roughly speaking, the course introduces stochastic calculus, Brownian motion and classical mathematical finance, such as delta-hedging and risk-neutral pricing via Girsanov's formula. A syllabus be found [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1XbfUs71N0bS5-R-SjdgX5NQmHtWAv8Xx/view).
